Feeling like you're constantly glued to your Google Ads campaigns? Managing strict budgets is stressful. Overspending even slightly? Not an option. But who has the time to monitor campaigns 24/7?

Here's a solution to help you stay in control without having to check your budgets each morning.

## What this script does

This Google Ads script:
- Monitors accounts for potential overspending (MCC-level)
- Provides a daily overview of budget spendings
- Sends you email updates every day

It's like having an extra set of eyes on your campaigns while you focus on other priorities. And the best part? You don't have to choose between control and balance.
