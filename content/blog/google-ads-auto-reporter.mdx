Save hours each month with this Google Ads script that automatically updates your reports.

When a new month begins, it always means I need to send out reports. This isn't necessarily my favorite task since manually gathering data from Google Ads has been time-consuming.

## The solution

Fortunately, I found a solution two years ago. Before it was popular to use ChatGPT for such tasks, I hired someone on Upwork to develop a Google Ads script that automatically sends data daily to a Google Spreadsheet.

## How it works

The script runs daily and pushes key campaign metrics directly into a Google Sheet. No manual exports, no copy-pasting, no mistakes.

From there, you can connect the sheet to Looker Studio for beautiful automated dashboards that update themselves.

## The result

What used to take hours of manual data gathering each month now happens automatically. Every morning, fresh data is waiting in my spreadsheet. Reports practically write themselves.
