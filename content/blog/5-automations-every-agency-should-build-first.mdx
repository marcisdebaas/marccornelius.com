You don't need 50 automations. You need 5 that actually save hours every week. Here's where to start.

## 1. Client onboarding

Every new client goes through the same steps. Welcome email, intake form, project setup, Slack channel creation, kickoff scheduling. Automate the sequence so it triggers the moment a contract is signed.

## 2. Weekly reporting

Stop pulling data manually from Google Ads, Analytics, and social platforms. Set up automated reports that compile, format, and send themselves every Monday morning.

## 3. Invoice generation

If your invoices follow a pattern (and they usually do), automate them. Pull hours from your time tracker, apply rates, generate the PDF, and send it.

## 4. Content repurposing

Write once, publish everywhere. A single blog post can become a LinkedIn post, email newsletter section, and Twitter thread — automatically.

## 5. Internal handoffs

When a project moves from sales to delivery, or from one team member to another, automate the notification, document sharing, and status update.

## Start here

Pick one. The one that annoys you most. Build it this week. Then move to the next.
