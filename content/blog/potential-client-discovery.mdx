What if you knew everything about a new client before the first call? No last-minute Googling. No digging through their website. Just a clean, instant summary.

That's exactly what I built with Make.

## How it works

Now, every time a new client reaches out, I get a background profile automatically:

- Their industry, company size & key details
- Insights pulled directly from their website
- All stored neatly in Notion for easy access

## The automation flow

1. **Mailhook** grabs incoming emails from potential clients
2. **HTTP Request** scans their website for background info
3. **Text Parsing** cleans it up for analysis
4. **OpenAI Module** summarizes key insights
5. **Notion Integration** stores it for quick access

## The result

No more manual research. No more wasted time. Just instant client intelligence.

And the best part? It took me minutes to set up.
