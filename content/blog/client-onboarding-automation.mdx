A walkthrough of how I automated client onboarding for a marketing agency doing €15k/month.

## The problem

Every time a new client signed, the same thing happened: 3 hours of manual setup. Create folders, send welcome emails, set up tools, schedule the kickoff call, share documents.

Multiply that by 4 new clients per month, and you're looking at 12 hours of pure busywork.

## The solution

I mapped the entire onboarding flow and identified every step that could be automated. The result: a single trigger (contract signed in the CRM) kicks off the entire process.

## What happens automatically now

1. Welcome email sends with all relevant links
2. Project folder is created in Google Drive
3. Slack channel is created and team members are invited
4. Intake form is sent to the client
5. Kickoff call is scheduled via Calendly
6. Internal task list is generated in the project management tool

## The result

Onboarding time went from 3 hours to 10 minutes. The remaining 10 minutes is the human touch — a personal welcome message and a quick review of the intake form.

## The takeaway

Not everything needs to be automated. But the predictable, repetitive parts? Those should run themselves.
