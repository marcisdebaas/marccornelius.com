PPC marketers, meet your new secret weapon: reviews. Not your reviews, but your competitors'.

Here's the deal: Your competitors are leaving breadcrumbs in their reviews — issues their customers face. Take advantage of it.

## What it does

I made a Custom GPT which helps you with this:

1. You enter a keyword and pick the country or language
2. It analyzes reviews from 10 competitors
3. You get a table of most common customer pain points
4. Then it spits out ads: 10 attention-grabbing headlines and 4 killer descriptions

All focused on issues the GPT has found about your competitors.

## How it works

- Scan reviews from competitors in seconds
- Pinpoint their 10 most common customer complaints
- Get Google Ads headlines that turn competitors' weaknesses into your strengths
