Most Custom GPTs are overachievers. Too many instructions = too many mistakes. The solution: A Chain of Specialized GPTs — a "train" with three focused GPTs, each handling one task exceptionally well.

## GPT 1: Negative Review Bender

Searches for negative reviews in your niche and transforms complaints into ad benefits. "People hate the slow delivery in your niche? Your ad now says: 'Fast shipping, never wait again.'"

What people hate reveals what they care about.

## GPT 2: Website Benefits Extractor

Paste GPT 1's output and your website URL. Extracts real benefits instead of features.

Not "4K Resolution" but "See every detail like never before." Not "AI-powered automation" but "Save 5+ hours a week with zero manual work."

## GPT 3: Cialdini's Persuasion Engine

Layers psychological principles of persuasion:
- **Scarcity:** "Only 5 left, grab yours now"
- **Authority:** "Used by 10,000+ professionals"
- **Social proof:** "Trusted by top companies"

## The result

The complete train produces 50+ killer headlines and descriptions ready for your next PPC campaign.
